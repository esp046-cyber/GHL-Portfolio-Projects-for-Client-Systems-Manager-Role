Webhook-Powered Lead Distribution System via Make

Make (Integromat) · Webhooks · Multi-account routing

Built a lead routing system for an agency managing multiple client sub-accounts. When a new lead submitted a form on any of the client websites, a Make scenario captured the webhook, identified the lead source and service type, and routed the contact to the correct GHL sub-account with the right tags, pipeline stage, and assigned team member. Removed the need for manual lead distribution entirely and ensured zero leads fell through the cracks. Also set up Slack notifications to alert account managers in real time.

AI-Assisted Lead Response System Using GHL + GPT

AI integration · Response automation · Conversation handling

Integrated OpenAI into a GHL workflow to power AI-assisted first responses for inbound leads. When a new lead messaged through the website chat widget, the system triggered an n8n workflow that passed the lead's message and context (service interest, source) to GPT-4, generated a personalized reply, and sent it back through GHL within seconds. Built a fallback rule so conversations flagged as high-intent were handed off to a live agent immediately. Helped the client handle 3x more inbound volume without adding headcount.

Reactivation Campaign System for Dormant Leads

Re-engagement · Segmented campaigns · Pipeline recovery

Designed a reactivation system targeting leads that had gone cold for 60+ days in a client's GHL account. Built a smart list to identify dormant contacts by last activity date and pipeline stage. Launched a 5-step re-engagement sequence mixing email and SMS with personalized messaging based on the lead's original interest. Contacts who engaged were automatically moved back into an active pipeline and flagged for follow-up. Contacts who remained unresponsive after the sequence were tagged as "unqualified" to keep the CRM clean. Recovered a meaningful percentage of previously lost leads within 30 days.
